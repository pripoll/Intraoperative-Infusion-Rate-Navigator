ADULT INTRAOPERATIVE INFUSION NAVIGATOR

This tool is intended for ADULT patients only. It calculates the infusion volume of isotonic fluids required to maintain normovolemia during surgery. It does not provide calculations for glucose or electrolyte requirements, which must be managed independently as additional supplements.

WARNING: Blood loss is not included in these calculations. It must be added to the suggested amounts, either at a 1:1 ratio with blood products or colloids, or at a 3:1 ratio if ISOTONIC crystalloids are used.
