ADULT INTRAOPERATIVE INFUSION NAVIGATOR

This tool is specifically designed for ADULT patients. It calculates the infusion volume of isotonic fluids required to maintain normovolemia during surgery. It does not provide calculations for glucose or electrolyte requirements, nor is it intended for pediatric use.

WARNING: Blood loss is not included. It must be added to suggested amounts, either at a 1:1 ratio with blood products/colloids or a 3:1 ratio with ISOTONIC crystalloids.
